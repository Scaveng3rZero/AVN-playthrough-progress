VN Playthrough Progress is a simple tracker showing where each series is in my production pipeline. If you’re a subscriber or patron, this is the fastest way to see what’s being recorded, voiced, edited, and published.
How to read the stages
• Not Started — I haven’t recorded the episode yet.
• Recorded — gameplay captured; not finalized for release.
• Voice Over Complete — voices are finished (including Sam/character work where applicable).
• Editing Done — editing is finished and queued for upload.
• Published to Patreon — live for patrons.
• Published to YouTube — live for everyone.
Pages on this site
• Progress — the tracker itself.
• Game Links — curated store/dev pages (18+).
• Recommend — submit suggestions for future playthroughs.
• About — you’re here.
